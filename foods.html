Healthy Snacks

  • Chicken - High in protein and helps build and maintain muscle mass.
  • Salmon - Rich in omega-3 fatty acids which promote healthy skin and coat.
  • Sweet Potatoes - Good source of fiber and vitamins, aids in digestion.
  • Carrots - Low in calories, high in vitamins, and helps with dental health.
  • Blueberries - Packed with antioxidants and vitamins that support overall health.
  • Apples (without seeds) - Provides vitamins and fiber; good for digestion.
  • Rice - Easy to digest and can be a good filler in a balanced diet.
  • Peanut Butter (in moderation) - Protein-rich and can be used as a treat or training aid.

Foods to Avoid

  • Chocolate - Contains theobromine, which is toxic to dogs.
  • Grapes and Raisins - Can cause kidney failure in dogs.
  • Onions and Garlic - Can lead to gastrointestinal upset and anemia.
  • Alcohol - Can be extremely dangerous and cause severe health issues.
  • Caffeine - Stimulates the nervous system and can be toxic.
  • Avocado - Contains persin, which can cause vomiting and diarrhea.
  • Macadamia Nuts - Can cause weakness, tremors, and hyperthermia.
