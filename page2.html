More Information

This section has additional details about Yoshi's breed, history, and some interesting facts that make her unique.

  • Breed: Shiba Inu
    • Native to Japan
    • Fox-like appearance
    • Dense double coat, erect ears, curled tail
  • History:
    • Bred for hunting small game in Japan's mountains
    • "Shiba Inu" means "brushwood dog"
    • Nearly went extinct during World War II, saved by breeders
  • Personality:
    • Independent and bold, often compared to cats
    • Intelligent and strong-willed, requires patient training
    • Loyal and affectionate with family
  • Unique Traits:
    • Shiba scream: High-pitched noise when excited or unhappy
    • Gait: "Springy trot" reflecting their hunting history
    • Agile and athletic build
